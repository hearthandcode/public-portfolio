## July 7, 2026: governance must not depend on model compliance

> A recorded design observation noted output-quality drift when switching among models. Routing and taxonomy conventions helped, but did not consistently enforce the intended result. The observation motivated exploration of validator-oriented governance rather than reliance on model compliance alone.

## July 12, 2026: legibility is a design constraint

> The initial Mermaid diagram did not render its newline labels legibly in Obsidian. The diagram was revised into compact single-line nodes with detail moved into a surrounding legend. A native Obsidian JSON Canvas was also created with text cards, file cards, groups, and labeled edges for the same ContextPack pathway.
